Product Overview:

POTASSIUM & MAGNESIUM contains potassium and magnesium, two vital minerals essential for the complete functioning of the human body. Potassium has an electrolytic function, balancing bodily fluids. The functions include also transfer of nerve impulses and muscle contraction. Magnesium contributes to protein synthesis, muscle contraction, and supports the immune system. Very useful for people performing intensive athletic activities. Deficiency of these two minerals can cause cramps and deteriorate performance capacity.

Key benefits

  • Combats muscle weakness and fatigue
  • Restrains muscle cramps
  • Avoids energy dip

Notice:

  •  For adults only and does not replace a varied balanced diet and healthy lifestyle.
  •  Do not exceed the recommended dose.
  •  Recommended dose : 3 caps per day
  •  Do not use it during breastfeeding, pregnancy periods without consulting the doctor.
  •  Store the product in a cool, dry place in a well sealed package out of reach for children under 3 years.
